Welcome to Test Counting Numbers!

Objective:

Listen to the sound and click the answer you think is the same number!

How to play:

Click on the play button (the button with a music note) to hear a number spoken in Myaamia. Make sure your speakers are on so you can hear it! Once you hear the sound, look at your choices for an answer and pick the one you think is right.

If the answer is right you will hear "good job" in Myaamia and see a green check mark! Otherwise the computer will say "try again" in Myaamia and display a red X.

Click the red Home Button at the top to return to the counting numbers home page!
