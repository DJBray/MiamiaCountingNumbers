Welcome to Try Counting Numbers

Instructions:

Learn the Myaamia Counting Numbers by clicking numbers and hitting play (the music note button)! To create a two digit number, click the number for the ten's place then the number for the one's place. The digits fill in left to right.

To clear the number, click the clear button at the bottom right of the application.

Click the red Home Button at the top to return to the counting numbers home page!
